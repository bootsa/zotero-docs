## Individual File Storage

Each Zotero user is given 300 MB of free Zotero File Storage, with larger storage plans available for purchase. To check your current usage and/or sign up for additional storage, visit the [storage settings](/settings/storage) of your Zotero account. You can then configure your storage settings through your Zotero client's [Sync preferences](preferences/sync).

Zotero File Storage services are provided by the [Corporation for Digital Scholarship](http://www.digitalscholar.org/).

### Storage Pricing

Zotero File Storage offers tiered pricing to accommodate a broad range of users.

| Storage Quota | Cost                                |
|---------------|-------------------------------------|
| 300 MB        | Free                                |
| 2 GB          | $20/year (equal to $1.67 per month) |
| 6 GB          | $60/year (equal to $5 per month)    |
| Unlimited [1] | $120/year (equal to $10 per month)  |

If you increase/decrease your storage subscription quota, your existing payment will be pro-rated based on the time remaining and then used to set a new expiration date for your new subscription level. For example, if you increase from 6 GB ($60/year) after 6 months to Unlimited ($120/year), your new subscription will expire 3 months in the future.

In addition to these individual storage plans, we also offer [institutional storage plans](institutional_storage).

### Using Zotero File Storage for Groups

No additional fees are charged for collaboration. Closed public and private [groups](groups) can use Zotero File Storage for file syncing as long as the group owner has storage space available and has enabled file editing in the group library settings. Group file storage always draws from the storage account of the group owner.
