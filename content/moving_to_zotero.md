# Importing from Other Reference Managers

It's easy to migrate your data from other reference management tools to Zotero. Instructions for popular tools are linked below.

-   [Mendeley](kb/mendeley_import)
-   [EndNote](kb/endnote_import)
-   [Citavi](kb/import_from_citavi)
-   [Microsoft Word Bibliography XML](kb/importing_formatted_bibliographies)
-   [Plain text reference lists](kb/importing_formatted_bibliographies)
-   [Bib(La)TeX](kb/importing_standardized_formats)
-   [JabRef](kb/importing_standardized_formats)

You can also import from other tools, such as Reference Manager, RefWorks, Papers, Google Scholar Library, ReadCube, etc., by exporting to a [standardized reference format](kb/importing_standardized_formats), such as RIS, BibTeX, or CSL JSON, and then importing into Zotero by clicking File → “Import…” and choose "A file".

If you already have data in a Zotero library on another computer, [follow these instructions](kb/transferring_a_library) to transfer your library to your new computer.
