### What version of Zotero do I have?

#### Zotero

You can check your Zotero version by selecting About Zotero from the Zotero menu (Mac) or Help menu (Windows/Linux). Make sure you have the latest version listed on the [changelog](5.0_changelog).

You can upgrade to the latest version of Zotero via Help -> "Check for Updates…" within Zotero or, if that's not working correctly, from the [download page](/download/).

#### Zotero for iOS / Android

Tap Back in the top-left corner until you get to the libraries screen, and then tap the gear icon in the top right. The version will be listed at the bottom of the settings screen.
