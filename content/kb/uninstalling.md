# How do I uninstall Zotero?

You can uninstall Zotero like any program on your computer. The [Zotero data directory](zotero_data) is not removed when you uninstall Zotero.

You can uninstall the Zotero Connector from your browser's Extensions pane.

To uninstall the Zotero word processor plugins, see [these instructions](kb/uninstalling_the_word_processor_plugin).
