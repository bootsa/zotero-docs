# Personal

Each Zotero user is given 100 MB of free Zotero File Storage, with [larger storage plans](/storage/storagerewrite/storage#storage_pricing) available for purchase. To sign up for additional storage, visit the [storage settings](/settings/storage) of your Zotero account profile and select a storage plan. You can then configure your storage settings through your Zotero [Sync preferences](preferences/sync). You can view your current Zotero File Storage usage and quota in your [Storage Settings](/settings/storage).

Zotero File Storage services are provided by the [Corporation for Digital Scholarship](http://www.digitalscholar.org/).  

### Storage Pricing
