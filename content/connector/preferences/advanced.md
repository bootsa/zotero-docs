##### Translators

Zotero Connector will automatically send an error report to zotero.org if saving a page fails. You can disable sending this error report by unchecking *Report broken site translators to zotero.org*.

The Connector will automatically keep translators up to date by either getting them from Zotero Standalone or directly from zotero.org. To perform a manual update you can click the Update Translators button. To reset and retrieve the latest version of translators stored in the Connector you can use the Reset Translators button.
